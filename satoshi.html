About Satoshi Isshiki

Satoshi Isshiki is the current 2nd seat in the Totsuki Council of 10.

Satoshi Isshiki is a young man with curly, medium-length brown hair.

Satoshi Isshiki is a 91st Totsuki Generation student and he is a resident of

the Polar Star Dormitory.

Characteristics about Satoshi Isshiki:

Satoshi Isshiki is the representative of the Polar Star Dormitory and best

embodies what the dorm represents.

He cares deeply for his fellow residents at the dorm, defending them when

when people look down on them.

He is very comical, silly and is a naturist, which often comes as a shock

for those who enter the Polar Star Dormitory for the first time.
