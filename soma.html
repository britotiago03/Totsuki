Soma Yukihira

About Soma Yukihira:

Soma Yukihira is the current 1st seat in the Totsuki Council of 10.

Soma Yukihira is a young teenage body with yellow eyes and a vertical

scar on his left eyebrow.

He is a 92nd Totsuki Generation student and is

a current resident of the Polar Star Dormitory.

Characteristics about Soma Yukihira:

Soma Yukihira is an energetic and optimistic boy who has the constant

drive to improve his cooking, mostly due to his strong relationship

and rivalry with his father, Joichiro Saiba

He is confident in his skills and always seeks to challenge others

to prove his abilities and test the limits of his cooking.

Soma's main outfit is his Restaurant Yukihira cooking uniform, consisting

of a black shirt with the restaurant's logo on the back and left chest area

of his shirt.
