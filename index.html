Welcome to Totsuki Academy

About Totsuki Academy:

Totsuki Academy is a famous culinary school located in Tokyo, Japan.

Totsuki boasts the best and the harshest culinary education in the world.

What is the Totsuki Council of 10?

The Totsuki Council of 10 is the student council of Totsuki Academy.

It is made up of the ten best chefs within Totsuki Academy.

The Totsuki Council of 10 Members:

  • 1st seat: Soma Yukihira
  • 2nd seat: Satoshi Isshiki
  • 3rd seat: Terunori Kuga
  • 4th seat: Akira Hayama
  • 5th seat: Ryo Kurokiba
  • 6th seat: Alice Nakiri
  • 7th seat: Takumi Aldini
  • 8th seat: Etsuya Eizan
  • 9th seat: Nene Kinokumi
  • 10th seat: Megumi Tadokoro
