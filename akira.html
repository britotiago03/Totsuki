About Akira Hayama

Akira Hayama is the current 4th seat in the Totsuki Council of 10.

He is proficient in the usage of spices and is known for his incredible

sense of smell and mastery of aroma.

Akira Hayama is a 92nd Totsuki Generation student and the winner of

the 43rd Annual Tōtsuki Autumn Election.

He was originally the 9th seat of the Totsuki Council of 10, but after

the Régiment de Cuisine, he became the current 4th seat.

Characteristics about Akira Hayama:

Akira is a confident and calm student in Totsuki due to his sharp

sense of smell which rivals Erina's Trump Tongue.

Akira proudly displays his ability and is not afraid to say that

he is the best chef in the school.

Like many of his classmates, Akira firmly believes that he will

stand on top of Totsuki thanks to his abilities and is one of the

few people who will stand up to Erina, declaring that he will claim her spot.
