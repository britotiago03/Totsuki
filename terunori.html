Terunori Kuga

About Terunori Kuga:

Terunori Kuga is the current 3rd seat in the Totsuki Council of 10.

Terunori is the shortest male Totsuki Council of 10 member and has a lean build.

He has slanted, wide brown eyes and wild, spiky dark brown hair that runs down to

his nape and juts in every direction.

Terunori Kuga is a 91st Totsuki Generation student and previously held the 8th seat

before moving up to 3rd seat in the Totsuki Council of 10.

He was also the president of the Chinese Cuisine RS.

Characteristics about Terunori Kuga:

Terunori is a loud but gleeful and sociable individual who immediately

warms up to people he just met.

He dislikes formalities and considers them a pain.

He is competitive and confident with his abilities, as he devotes himself

in defeating the other people above him.

The most notable trait is his sensitivity towards his height, becoming

extremely angry when someone mentions or ridicules it.
